RULE-001: HIGH_AMOUNT_VELOCITY
If a user submits more than 5 transactions totaling over $2000 within a 1-hour window, flag for review.
Risk level: HIGH. This pattern is consistent with account takeover or stolen card rapid liquidation.

RULE-002: UNUSUAL_HOUR_LARGE_AMOUNT
Transactions above $500 between 01:00-05:00 AM local time trigger elevated scrutiny.
Risk level: MEDIUM. Most legitimate large purchases do not occur in late-night hours.

RULE-003: CROSS_BORDER_VELOCITY
Two or more transactions from different countries within 2 hours for the same account.
Risk level: HIGH. Physical travel is impossible in this timeframe; indicates credential sharing or card cloning.

RULE-004: NEW_DEVICE_HIGH_VALUE
First-time device fingerprint combined with transaction amount exceeding the user's 95th percentile.
Risk level: MEDIUM-HIGH. New devices with atypical spend warrant secondary verification.

RULE-005: ROUND_AMOUNT_PATTERN
Three or more consecutive round-dollar transactions (e.g., $100.00, $200.00) within 24 hours.
Risk level: LOW-MEDIUM. Round amounts are common in card-testing (carding) fraud attempts.

RULE-006: MERCHANT_CATEGORY_MISMATCH
Transaction in high-risk merchant categories (crypto, wire transfer, gambling) from users with no prior history in those categories.
Risk level: MEDIUM. Sudden category change is a behavioural anomaly.

RULE-007: MULTI_IP_SESSION
More than 3 distinct IP addresses used by one account in a single session.
Risk level: HIGH. May indicate proxy/VPN rotation or session hijacking.

RULE-008: CREDIT_UTILIZATION_SPIKE
Credit utilization exceeds 90% within 48 hours for an account previously below 40%.
Risk level: MEDIUM. Rapid drawdown is a known precursor to default and bust-out fraud.

RULE-009: REPEATED_DECLINED_THEN_APPROVED
More than 3 declines followed by an approval within 30 minutes on the same account.
Risk level: MEDIUM-HIGH. May indicate card-stuffing or retrying with stolen credentials.

RULE-010: PRIOR_FRAUD_HISTORY
Any account with prior confirmed fraud within 12 months receives a baseline risk uplift of +0.2.
Risk level: Context-dependent. Prior fraud is the single strongest predictor of future fraud.

POLICY-A: STEP_UP_AUTHENTICATION
Accounts flagged with risk score >= 0.7 must complete MFA before the transaction proceeds.

POLICY-B: MANUAL_REVIEW_QUEUE
Transactions with risk score in [0.5, 0.7) are routed to the analyst review queue with a 4-hour SLA.

POLICY-C: AUTO_BLOCK
Transactions with risk score >= 0.9 are automatically declined and the account is temporarily suspended pending investigation.

POLICY-D: SAR_FILING
Confirmed fraud losses exceeding $5,000 require a Suspicious Activity Report filed within 30 days per BSA/AML compliance.
